Course Description

Multimedia involves the use of a combination of media such as sound, video, film, animation, graphics and artwork in the production of graphic design, computer games, websites or advertisements. This full-time course aims to introduce students to the use of multimedia computing in a range of design and production areas. Students gain skills in industry computer packages such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Animate, Adobe Dreamweaver and Adobe Premier Pro.
